How to Check Your Coin:

1. Flip the coin to the Reverse side (Tails).

2. Find the Year of issue.

3. Look specifically at the space below the year. This is the most common location for mint marks on Republic India coins.

Note: For older British India coins, marks may be hidden in the floral decoration.
